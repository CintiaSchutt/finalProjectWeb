Natal Luz

At the end of the year, Gramado city, Rio Grande do Sul, is covered in lights and colors to receive one of the most anticipated dates of the year: Christmas. Children and adults go into ecstasies over more than 300 attractions during the season, among shows, parades, musical performances and much more.

There are several free shows, such as the Christmas Parade, the Reindeer Exhibit, and the Singing Tree. The Lighting Show is one of the must-see events. In it, you are invited to use your Christmas memories to light up the Christmas tree and the city.
